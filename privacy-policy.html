Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 November 2025

ScubaSpots (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the ScubaSpots mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed by Supabase)
  • Optional profile information

1.2 Device Information

To ensure security and app performance, we may collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • Approximate location (if enabled for map-based features)
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, and usage analytics

1.3 User-Generated Content

If you upload content (photos, reviews, site suggestions), this information is stored securely in our Supabase database.

1.4 Authentication Information

ScubaSpots uses Supabase Authentication. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow login across multiple devices
  • Provide dive site listings, photos, and reviews
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and security
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity

3. Aggregated & Anonymised Data

We may analyse or process data in an aggregated or anonymised form to improve the app and for industry insights. Aggregated or anonymised data cannot be used to identify you.

Examples include:

  • Total number of dives logged in a region
  • General usage patterns (without identifying individuals)

4. Sharing of Anonymised or Aggregated Data

We may share anonymised or aggregated data with trusted partners for purposes such as:

  • Industry research
  • Marketing insights
  • Environmental or tourism analysis
  • Commercial partnerships

This data does not include personal information such as your name, email address, account details, or identifiable photos.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share anonymised data only when it cannot be linked back to an individual.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • Data is securely stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL + Storage)
  • All communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access is restricted through secure tokens and role-based permissions

6. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service.

8. Children's Privacy
