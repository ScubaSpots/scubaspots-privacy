Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 November 2025

ScubaSpots (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the ScubaSpots mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed by Supabase)
  • Optional profile information

1.2 Device Information

To ensure security and app functionality, we may collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • Approximate location (if enabled for map-based features)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

1.3 Dive Site Content (User-Generated)

If you add content (reviews, photos, site suggestions), this information is stored securely in our Supabase database.

1.4 Authentication Information

ScubaSpots uses Supabase Authentication. No passwords or raw credentials are ever transmitted or stored by ScubaSpots directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To allow you to log in across multiple devices
  • To display dive locations, photos, and reviews
  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • All data is securely stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL + Storage)
  • All network transfers use HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access is restricted through secure authentication tokens

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We may share information only when required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at:

scubaspots@outlook.com

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Links to Other Services

ScubaSpots may link to external websites or content. We are not responsible for external privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available at this URL.
