## What Data Will Be Deleted?

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

- Your ScubaSpots account  
- Your profile information  
- Your reviews and ratings  
- Photos you have uploaded  
- Any activity logs associated with your account  
- Saved locations or preferences  

---

## Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

For security, fraud prevention and audit requirements, some data may be retained for up to **30 days**, including:

- Server logs  
- System-level device information  
- Anonymised records that cannot identify you  

These records cannot be used to identify any user and are automatically purged after the retention period.
